Features of careChain-NFT ?

Offers

Offering NFT-based healthcare access tokens for priority scheduling, discounted prices on labs/diagnostic tests, VIP/concierge services, and exclusive healthcare experiences

Xaman Integration

Integration with Xaman (formerly Xumm wallet) will provide secure storage and management of NFT assets, enhancing convenience and accessibility for participants

Benefits

Issuing NFTs(XRPL) as proof of participation in health and wellness programs/ medical research studies

Verification

Using NFTs to certify medical record, test results, referrals, etc. to ensure authenticity and accurate healthcare planning

Step 01

The NFTs will be minted on the XRPL and held/verified through the Xaman Wallet (previously known as Xumm wallet)

Step 02

Each NFT will be associated with an individual XRPL address for accurate verification of identity (possibly interoperable with SigVerify)
